Privacy Policy

Alert Me App

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

At Alert Me, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate and improve our services. This may include:

  • Account and Device Information: such as your Apple ID (for subscriptions), device type, and operating system.
  • Usage Data: anonymous statistics about app features you use, error logs, and performance metrics.
  • Optional Information: if you choose to contact support or provide feedback, we may receive your email or message details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide app functionality (alerts, insights, notifications)
  • Process and manage subscriptions via Apple In-App Purchase
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Respond to your support requests or inquiries

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your data. We do not store sensitive personal information on our servers, and subscription payments are securely processed through Apple’s App Store system.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share limited technical data with trusted analytics providers to improve performance, always in compliance with Apple’s privacy standards.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may link to third-party APIs or data sources (e.g., financial data providers, news APIs) strictly for content delivery. We do not control these third-party services, and their use is governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

7. Your Choices and Rights

You may delete the App or request data removal by contacting us. Subscription and billing data can be managed through your Apple ID account settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected by the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.
